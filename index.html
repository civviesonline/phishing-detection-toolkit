24-Hour Threat Analysis

Circadian is a 24-hour phishing detection tool for links, emails, QR codes, and attachments.

Use Circadian is a phishing detection tool, link safety checker, and email scam checker to investigate suspicious activity directly in the browser. The app helps teams detect phishing links, review risky messages, and train users with guided awareness modules.

How it works

  1. Paste a suspicious link, email, QR code, or attachment indicator into the matching scanner.
  2. Review the threat score, phishing indicators, and safety verdict returned by Circadian
  3. Use the analyst insights and awareness tools to decide whether to block, report, or escalate.

What Circadian checks

Circadian by PhishGuard helps detect phishing links, uncover spoofed sender behavior, inspect suspicious QR destination.
